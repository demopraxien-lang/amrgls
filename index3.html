Precision Science

Every Fracture Tells a Story

AGR's fracture analysis methods decode the origin, cause, and path of glass failure—turning each break into a roadmap for product improvement and liability defense.

Our investigators combine microscopy, stress analysis, and 50 years of accumulated pattern knowledge to deliver findings that hold up in court and on the production floor.

  • Residual stress & annealing analysis
  • Stone, cord & seed identification
  • Coating adhesion & chemical resistance
  • USP / EP pharmaceutical compliance
  • Heavy metal and contamination tracing
